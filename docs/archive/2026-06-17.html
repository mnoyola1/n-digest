RPA + AI

PreAct compiles repeat UI agent runs into state machines for 8.5-13x speedup

This is the exact pattern your CCB billing-automation flows need. Most RPA-plus-LLM work pays full inference cost on every run of a known task. PreAct compiles a successful trajectory into a deterministic replay with screen checks, then hands back to the agent only on drift. Worth modeling against your current UiPath and Power Automate token spend before the next vendor review.

The paper shows fine-tuning on AgentNet's 22K human traces actually degrades UI-TARS from 26% to 8% on OSWorld, while their synthetic pipeline lifts it. For the AI and Technology team, the lesson is procurement-grade: if a vendor pitches a computer-use agent trained on hand-collected traces, ask what synthetic distillation they ran.
