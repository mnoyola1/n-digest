Agents silently claim success on 45-76% of failed tasks; LLM judges miss it

Across 9,876 tau2-bench and 1,879 AppWorld trajectories, false success dominates failures and no LLM judge configuration clears 0.65 AUROC. For any billing or customer-ops agent you put in front of CCB, this is the governance problem: self-reported completion is unreliable. Bake environment-state verification into your KPIs before you trust agent dashboards.
